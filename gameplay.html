Tutorial

Welcome to News Tycoon! You're taking on the roll of Misinformation Officer of your very own misinformation campaign. Your goal is to spread the fake or misleading news as as far and as wide as possible. How? Easy! How to play:

  • You don't have a huge budget, so start off by clicking that newspaper. Each click is a piece of misinformation spread, and a dollar earned.
  • Save your points to purchase access to the quizzes. The quizzes start cheap and get more expensive as you go. But don't worry! It is worth it. Successful completion of additional misinformation training earns you points and even increases the spread of your misinformation campaign - more points per click!
  • You can also save up to hire managers to help you run your campaign. Each manager will help you click the button ever few seconds. They're a bit slow, but some help is better than no help, right?
  • That's all for now. Bottom line: Earn points, do some quizzes, earn even more points, and do more quizzes! Enjoy :)

About Page

Hi there! We're a group of BCIT CST students who set out to make an educational game about misinformation. Our goal was to make it educational, fun, and addictive to keep player's coming back for more. We hope you like it! In creating this game, we relied on Bruce Bartlett’s book "The Truth Matters: A Citizen’s Guide to Separating Facts from Lies and Stopping Fake News in Its Tracks" to assist us in providing helpful tips and questions through our quiz. From the DTC-10 (Cody, Joey, and Wilson) - Thanks for playing!
